About Mitchtin Foods

Mitchtin Foods is a leading agricultural company specializing in sweet potato and groundnut production through innovative contract farming partnerships. We are committed to empowering farmers while ensuring consistent, high-quality crop production for the market.


Our business model is built on mutual benefit - we provide farmers with essential inputs including fertilizers, herbicides, and insecticides, while guaranteeing a ready market for their produce. This approach ensures sustainable income for farmers and reliable supply chains for our customers.


With years of experience in agricultural value chains, we understand the challenges faced by smallholder farmers and have developed comprehensive solutions that address these needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality and sustainability.
